OUR VISION

The vision of the company is to use social network dataset to accurately predict depression and to help people to reduce it. Our aim is to predict illness at an early stage and alert the user about it and counsel them to foster mental wellness. We strive to achieve the best and most accurate predictions so that the intensity and possible impact is predicted and lives are saved.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide support to those in need. We aim to provide the perfect balance between anonymity and easy accessibility. Who understand you and guide you through completely confidential individual sessions.
